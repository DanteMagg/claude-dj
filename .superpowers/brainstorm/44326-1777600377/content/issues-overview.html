Claude DJ — Issues to Resolve

Before we redesign, let's align on what's broken and what we're fixing. Select all the issues that feel most critical to you.

1

Transitions don't actually happen

Song 1 played indefinitely — UI showed deck B as "ready" but the actual audio never crossfaded into Song 2. The mix script existed but wasn't executing in the audio stream.

2

UX / design is unclear

Hard to tell what state the app is in. What's playing? What's coming next? What is Claude doing right now? The visual hierarchy doesn't communicate the two-deck model clearly.

3

No Claude reasoning visible

Claude plans every transition and picks every track — but none of that thinking is surfaced. Users have no idea why a song was chosen or how the transition was designed.

4

Status indicators are confusing

"Planning", "Analyzing", "Loading", "Ready" — these labels appear and disappear with no clear meaning. Progress during the ~50s wait is opaque.

5

Two-deck model isn't visible

Real DJ software shows two turntables. There's no intuitive representation of "Deck A playing, Deck B preparing". The crossfader and channel concept is absent.

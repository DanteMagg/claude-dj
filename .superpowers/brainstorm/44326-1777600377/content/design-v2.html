Waveform

Canvas · frequency-colored

Rendered on canvas at 2px-per-bar density. Low freq bars warm (orange), mids neutral (white), highs cool (teal). Top/bottom mirrored, smooth amplitude via weighted moving average.

Density

Cockpit mode (density 8/10)

No card containers — only 1px dividers. Monospace for all numbers. Sub-12px type for labels. Everything is a single, unified data surface with no wasted chrome.

Status

Always readable at a glance

Deck A badge: green + pulse dot. Deck B cycles ANALYZING (amber) → PLANNING (amber) → LOADING (amber) → READY (blue) with color semantics, not just text.
